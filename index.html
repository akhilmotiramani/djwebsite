About Me

It all started in 2019. I was searching for an activity to fill my free time and came across a YouTube tutorial on how to mix music. I decided to buy myself a DJ controller and started to learn how to mix Bollywood as well as English music. After a few years and many different occasions, I decided to officially launch my own business. Now, I am a full-time student at UNC studying Computer Science and pursuing my side-business of DJing. My goal is to bring people of many different backgrounds together to enjoy the fruits of music. No matter your culture or language, anyone can groove to the beats of music, and I hope to spread this to the Raleigh/Durham and Charlotte area!
